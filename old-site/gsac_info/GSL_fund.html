Donate to the ChemE Graduate Student Life Fund

A fund dedicated to ChemE graduate student life was created in the College of Chemistry in 2009. This fund is put towards graduate student socials, student invited speakers, and other activities to improve the atmosphere for graduate students. An online form is available to donate to this fund.

Additionally, there is currently a New Alumni Challange that matches any gifts up to $1,000 from alumni in the classes of 2007-2011 (plus all current students who graduate in spring 2012 are eligible). Make sure to mark the New Alumni Challange check box under the Cal Affiliation heading. This is a great opportunity to support your alma mater and take advantage of the matching funds.
