[edit] LinkedIn Group

We have a LinkedIn Group for UC Berkeley Chemical Engineering alumni and current graduate students. This is a great way for current students and alumni to interact, and for alumni to recruit Berkeley graduate students to their companies. LinkedIn also has tools for filtering job postings based on your professional area of interest.

[edit] Job List Emails

Several email lists provide information about upcoming recruiting information sessions.

The primary list is associated with Callisto, UC Berkeley's password-protected career center site. All events, information sessions, and career fairs will be listed in Callisto.

The graduate coordinator will also send out announcements on the departmental distribution lists when companies that express particular interest in chemical engineering graduate students are hosting information sessions and interviews.

[edit] Career Center Resources

The Berkeley career center hosts a series of presentations and other events and resources targeted at graduate students. For more information, see the Career Center page for graduate students.
