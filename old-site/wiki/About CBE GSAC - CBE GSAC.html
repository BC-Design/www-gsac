[edit] About GSAC

Welcome to the website of the the Graduate Student Advisory Committee (GSAC) of the Department of Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering at University of California, Berkeley. Our goal is to improve the graduate life experience of students in this department. We provide input and assistance with departmental functions and policies that affect the graduate student body. Some of our activities include:

  • Organize and run monthly socials (ChemE kegs, Broomball, etc.)
  • Invite colloquium speakers for the Science and Society Student-invited speaker series
  • Organize industry-related events (panels, info sessions, etc.)
  • Organize department holiday parties, graduation parties, picnics, and sports events
  • Compile data on housing costs for students and lobbying for pay raises
  • Help recruiting new graduate students
  • Provide input on curriculum changes
  • Community outreach
  • Maintain Graduate Student Lounge
  • Distribute information from the department to all the labs

There are other projects that we can begin, but we are limited by our size. GSAC is a volunteers organization, so if you are interested in helping out, just send an email to cbe.gsac AT berkeley.edu and you will be added to our email list! We meet about once a month for an hour to an hour and a half. If you have any ideas on things we can do to make graduate student life better, please let us know—we will do our best to implement them.

[edit] Contact Us

Please feel free to contact us for more information or if you are interested in doing an event with us. We maintain a email address, although you may get a faster response by contacting a member directly.
