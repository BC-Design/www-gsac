Chemical & Biomolecular Engineering Graduate Student Information

This section contains much of the important information to get you through your degree. Please use the navigation on the left or at the top to get to the information you are looking for.

If you have additional questions, please send an email to our GSAC account (cbe.gsac@berkeley.edu) or to the Graduate Student Advisor (chegrad@cchem.berkeley.edu).

Graduate Student Handbook

Currently, the graduate student program information and policies are found on the CBE Graduate Student Information website and accessible via different links. The consolidated graduate student handbook (paper version) is handed to incoming students at the start of the graduate program. New students will continue to receive a hard copy of the graduate student handbook at the new student orientation.

You can always check the Graduate Student Handbook for official information about the program.
